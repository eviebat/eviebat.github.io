Preparing Tempeh - Steaming

Ingredients

  • Tempeh
  • Vegetable Broth

Instructions:

  • Slice tempeh into half inch thick slices
  • Submerge tempeh in vegetable broth in a saucepan and bring to a boil.
  • Once boiling, reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  • Remove tempeh and proceed with cooking
